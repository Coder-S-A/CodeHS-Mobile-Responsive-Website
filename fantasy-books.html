Fantasy Books

Keeper of the Lost Cities

Keeper of the Lost Cities is an 8 book long series which is still being written about a twelve year old high school senior named Sophie Foster, who, also happens to be a telepath. No one know this, but that changes when a mysterious boy named Fitz Vacker who can also read minds approaches her. She finds out that there is a place where she belongs, but she must leave the world she knows behind. This book is great for kids aged 9 to 12 who are looking for an escape to a magical world.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a 7 book long series that has been made into 8 movies. It is about an 11 year old boy named Harry who is a wizard. Throughout this story Harry finds truths about himslef and others which he could never have imagined to be true. With betrayals and friendships, this is the story of the adventures which Harry goes on to help save the wizarding world from destruction. It is a classic fantasy read and from three-headed-dogs to evil sorcerers, this story entertains parents and kids alike.

The School for Good and Evil

The School for Good and Evil is a 5 book long series which is still being written. The story is about to girls named Sophie and Agatha, who will be kidnapped to go to the School for Good and Evil. Sophie, with her undeniable beauty seems perfect for the School for Good, while Agatha with her with goth-like attitude, seems a natural fit for the School for Evil. However, when the two girls are swept off to the school, there fates are reversed, with Agatha in the School for Good and Sophie in the School for Evil.
